Story about Peacocks...

  • Peacock is one of the most beautiful birds in the world.
  • Peacock is the National Bird of India.
  • The Peacock is generally found in Asia.
  • There are mainly 2 types of peacock lives in the World.

Story about Owls...

  • An owl is a bird that hates the light.
  • It has a strong night vision and hunts in the dead of night.
  • It eats insects and small birds.
  • It has a very horrible looking face.
